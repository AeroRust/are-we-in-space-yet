YANP - Yet Another NMEA Parser.

A no_std Rust NMEA 0183 sentence parser.
Currently supported Sentences -> BOD, BWC, GBS, GGA, GLL, GSA, GNS, GSV, HDT, RMA, RMB, RMC, STN, VBW, VTG, WPL.
